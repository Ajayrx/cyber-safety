Common Online Scams

Be vigilant and protect yourself against common scams in the digital world.

Types of Scams

Phishing

Deceptive emails or messages that trick you into sharing sensitive information.

Malware

Malicious software designed to harm your device or steal personal data.

Social Engineering

Psychological manipulation to gain access to confidential information.

Impersonation Scams

Fraudsters pretending to be legitimate entities to steal your trust and money.
