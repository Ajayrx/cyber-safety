Protect Yourself from Senior Cyber Scams

Follow these steps to stay safe online and avoid falling victim to cyber scams.

Steps to Protect Yourself

Stay Informed

Be aware of common scams targeting seniors and keep updated with the latest cybersecurity trends.

Secure Your Devices

Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on your accounts and devices.

Verify Sources

Always verify the legitimacy of phone calls, emails, and messages before responding or providing personal information.

Report Suspicious Activity

If you receive a suspicious message or call, report it to authorities immediately to prevent further scams.
